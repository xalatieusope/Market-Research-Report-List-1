<p><strong>The Mammography Imaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Analog</li><li>Digital</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mammography imaging market comprises two main types: analog and digital. Analog mammography utilizes conventional film-screen systems to capture images of breast tissue, relying on chemical processing for image development. In contrast, digital mammography employs electronic detectors to convert X-ray photons into digital images, offering enhanced image quality and the ability for immediate viewing and manipulation. Digital systems can reduce radiation exposure and improve diagnostic accuracy, contributing to their growing prevalence in clinical settings.</p></p>
