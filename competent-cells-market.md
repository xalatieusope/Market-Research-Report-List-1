<p><strong>The Competent Cells Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chemically Competent Cells</li><li>Electrocompetent Cells</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Competent cells are bacterial cells engineered to take up foreign DNA, primarily used in molecular biology for cloning and transformation. Chemically competent cells are prepared using a chemical treatment, often with calcium chloride, which makes the cell membrane permeable to DNA. Electrocompetent cells, on the other hand, are subjected to an electrical field, allowing for more efficient DNA uptake. Both types serve distinct purposes in research and biotechnology, with variations in efficiency, transformation rates, and applications in genetic engineering.</p></p>
