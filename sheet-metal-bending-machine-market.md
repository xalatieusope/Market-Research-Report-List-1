<p><p>The sheet metal bending machine market includes three primary types: manual folding machines, CNC folding machines, and hydraulic folding machines. Manual folding machines require operator skill for bending processes, typically suited for small-scale jobs. CNC folding machines use computer programming for precision and efficiency, catering to complex designs and large production runs. Hydraulic folding machines utilize hydraulic pressure for powerful bending, making them ideal for thicker materials and heavy-duty applications. Each type serves distinct needs based on production scale and complexity.</p></p>
