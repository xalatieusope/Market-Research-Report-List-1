<p><p>The Peripheral Angiography Equipment Market encompasses various imaging technologies used to visualize blood vessels. Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) employs strong magnetic fields and radio waves to create detailed images of vessels. Computed Tomography (CT) uses X-ray data to produce cross-sectional images, enhancing vascular assessment. Ultrasound utilizes sound waves to visualize blood flow and vessel structure non-invasively. Nuclear Imaging involves radioactive tracers for functional insights into blood circulation. X-ray imaging remains a standard method for real-time vascular assessments in peripheral angiography.</p></p>
