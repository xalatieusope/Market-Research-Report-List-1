<p><strong>The Solids Flow Measuring Instruments Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contacting Measuring Instruments</li><li>Contactless Measuring Instruments</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solids flow measuring instruments market is categorized into contacting and contactless measuring instruments. Contacting measuring instruments involve direct physical interaction with the material, providing accurate measurements of flow rates and characteristics through devices like load cells and rotary valves. In contrast, contactless measuring instruments utilize non-invasive techniques, such as laser or radar technology, to gauge flow without physical contact, minimizing wear and contamination risks. Both types cater to various industrial applications, contributing to efficient material handling and processing.</p></p>
