<p><p>The Cubic Boron Nitride (CBN) abrasive market is primarily divided into two types: CBN Monocrystalline and CBN Micro Mist. CBN Monocrystalline features a single crystal structure, offering superior hardness and wear resistance, making it ideal for precision grinding and machining of hard materials. On the other hand, CBN Micro Mist consists of finely dispersed CBN particles in a mist form, providing effective cooling and lubrication during cutting processes. Both types cater to diverse industrial applications, enhancing efficiency and performance.</p></p>
