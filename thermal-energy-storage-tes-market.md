<p><strong>The Thermal Energy Storage (TES) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sensible Heat Regenerative</li><li>Latent Heat Storage</li><li>Thermochemical Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermal Energy Storage (TES) involves storing excess thermal energy for later use, enhancing energy efficiency. Sensible heat storage uses materials that absorb and release heat as their temperature changes, commonly found in water tanks. Latent heat storage utilizes phase change materials that absorb or release energy during phase transitions, offering high storage density. Thermochemical storage employs reversible chemical reactions to store and release heat, providing high energy density and long-term storage potential. Each type serves diverse applications in energy management.</p></p>
