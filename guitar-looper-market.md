<p><p>The guitar looper market can be categorized into two main types: floor-based and desktop-based loopers. Floor-based loopers are designed for live performances, featuring robust pedals that musicians can easily control while playing. They often come with multiple footswitches for hands-free operation. In contrast, desktop loopers are more suited for studio environments, offering advanced features and extensive editing capabilities. These devices usually connect to computers or other gear, allowing for detailed sound manipulation and production enhancements. Both types cater to different user needs and scenarios.</p></p>
