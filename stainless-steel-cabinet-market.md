<p><p>The stainless steel cabinet market is primarily segmented into two key types: 201 and 304 stainless steel. Type 201, known for its lower nickel content, is more cost-effective and offers good resistance to corrosion and oxidation but may not be as durable as type 304. Type 304, often referred to as food-grade stainless steel, boasts superior corrosion resistance and strength, making it ideal for commercial kitchens and environments where hygiene is crucial. Both types cater to varied consumer needs and applications.</p></p>
