<p><ul><li>IP Video Surveillance</li><li>Analog Video Surveillance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The video surveillance solutions market comprises primarily two types: IP video surveillance and analog video surveillance. IP video surveillance utilizes internet protocol to transmit digital video data, offering higher resolution, remote access, and advanced features like analytics and integration with other systems. In contrast, analog video surveillance relies on traditional coaxial cabling to transmit analog signals, typically resulting in lower image quality and limited scalability. Both serve distinct needs, with IP systems favored for modern applications and analog systems still prevalent in cost-sensitive environments.</p></p>
