<p><p>The signal converter market comprises primarily two types: Analog-to-Digital Converters (ADCs) and Digital-to-Analog Converters (DACs). ADCs transform analog signals, such as sound or temperature, into digital form for processing by computers and digital devices. Conversely, DACs convert digital data back into analog signals, enabling playback in speakers or other analog systems. These converters are essential in various industries, including telecommunications, consumer electronics, and automotive, facilitating seamless interaction between analog and digital systems.</p></p>
