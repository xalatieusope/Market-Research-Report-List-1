AF VCM is designed to adjust the focus of camera lenses in smartphones and digital cameras, ensuring sharp images. Meanwhile, OIS VCM stabilizes the image by compensating for small movements and vibrations, enhancing image clarity in varying conditions. Both technologies are essential for improving photography and videography experiences in consumer electronics, driving demand and innovation in the market.</p></p>
