<p><p>The mobile email market comprises three main types: transactional email, acquisition email, and retention email. Transactional emails are triggered by user actions, such as confirmations or receipts, to facilitate ongoing communication. Acquisition emails aim to attract new customers, offering incentives or valuable content to encourage sign-ups. Retention emails focus on re-engaging existing users, promoting loyalty through personalized offers or updates. Together, these email types enhance user experience and drive business growth by fostering communication at different customer journey stages.</p></p>
