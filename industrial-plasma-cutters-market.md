<p><p>The industrial plasma cutters market is divided into two primary types: manual and CNC (Computer Numerical Control). Manual plasma cutters are operated by skilled technicians who manually control the cutting process, providing flexibility for diverse applications. In contrast, CNC plasma cutters are automated systems that use computer controls to precisely cut materials, enhancing efficiency and accuracy for large-scale production. Both types serve distinct needs, with manual cutters ideal for smaller tasks and CNC models suited for intricate, high-volume cutting operations.</p></p>
