<p><strong>The Self Contained Breathing Apparatus (SCBA) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open-Circuit SCBA</li><li>Closed-Circuit SCBA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Self-Contained Breathing Apparatus (SCBA) is vital for providing breathable air in hazardous environments. The market is primarily divided into two types: Open-Circuit SCBA and Closed-Circuit SCBA. Open-Circuit SCBA releases exhaled air into the environment and features a high-pressure air cylinder. It’s widely used in firefighting and rescue operations. Conversely, Closed-Circuit SCBA recirculates exhaled air through a CO2 scrubber, offering longer duration and reduced air consumption, ideal for confined space work and military applications. Each type serves distinct operational needs.</p></p>
