<p><p>The cardiopulmonary bypass system market is primarily divided into two main types: conventional and mini cardiopulmonary bypass systems. Conventional systems involve large equipment that temporarily takes over the functions of the heart and lungs during surgery, allowing surgeons to perform complex procedures. In contrast, mini cardiopulmonary bypass systems are designed for minimally invasive surgeries, offering a more compact and portable solution that reduces patient trauma and recovery time. Both systems play a crucial role in enhancing surgical outcomes and patient safety.</p></p>
