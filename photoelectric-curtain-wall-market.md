<p><p>The Photoelectric Curtain Wall market comprises two main types: Single-Layered Photovoltaic Curtain Walls and Double-Layered Photovoltaic Curtain Walls. Single-Layered systems integrate photovoltaic cells within a single glazing layer, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, Double-Layered systems feature an additional layer of glazing, enhancing thermal insulation and efficiency while allowing for greater energy generation. Both types serve as functional building facades that harness solar energy, contributing to sustainable architecture and energy-efficient building designs.</p></p>
