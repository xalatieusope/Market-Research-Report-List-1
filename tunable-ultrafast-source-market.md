<p><p>The tunable ultrafast source market primarily includes femtosecond and picosecond laser sources, essential for various scientific and industrial applications. Femtosecond laser sources produce pulses on the order of femtoseconds (10^-15 seconds), enabling high-resolution imaging and precision material processing. In contrast, picosecond laser sources generate longer pulses, typically in the picosecond range (10^-12 seconds), suitable for applications like telecommunications and medical technologies. Both types are crucial for advancements in fields such as spectroscopy, nanotechnology, and laser surgery, driving significant market growth.</p></p>
