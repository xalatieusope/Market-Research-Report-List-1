<p><p>The handheld illuminometer market encompasses two primary types: digital and analog illuminometers. Digital illuminometers are equipped with electronic sensors that provide precise, easily readable measurements of light intensity, often featuring data logging and advanced display options. In contrast, analog illuminometers utilize mechanical components and scales to measure light, offering a straightforward, battery-free alternative. Both types are crucial for applications in photography, industrial settings, and environmental studies, enabling users to assess lighting conditions effectively.</p></p>
