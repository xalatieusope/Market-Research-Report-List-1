<p><ul><li>Main Landing Gear</li><li>Nose Landing Gear</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The landing gear market is primarily divided into two main types: main landing gear and nose landing gear. Main landing gear supports the bulk of the aircraft's weight during landing and takeoff, typically located under the wings or fuselage. Nose landing gear, on the other hand, is positioned at the front of the aircraft, providing balance and steering control during ground operations. Both types are critical for aircraft safety and performance, with advancements focused on weight reduction and efficiency.</p></p>
