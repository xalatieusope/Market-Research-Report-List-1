<p><p>The stainless steel high-pressure boiler tube market comprises several types, including ferritic, austenitic, martensitic, and others. Ferritic stainless steel features a high resistance to stress corrosion and is often used in moderate temperatures. Austenitic stainless steel offers superior ductility and resistance to corrosion, making it suitable for high-temperature applications. Martensitic stainless steel is known for its strength and ability to withstand high pressures, while "other" categories may include duplex and super duplex stainless steels, which combine features of the primary types.</p></p>
