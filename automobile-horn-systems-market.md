<p><p>The automobile horn systems market primarily consists of two types: air horns and electric horns. Air horns operate using compressed air, producing a loud, resonant sound often used in vehicles like trucks and buses for their attention-grabbing volume. In contrast, electric horns generate sound through an electromagnetic mechanism, offering a more compact design and varied tones, making them common in passenger cars. Both types serve essential safety functions in alerting pedestrians and other drivers on the road.</p></p>
