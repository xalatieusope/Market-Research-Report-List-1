<p><ul><li>High Temperature Vulcanization(HTV)</li><li>Room Temperature Vulcanization(RTV)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The silicone rubber market is categorized into two main types: High Temperature Vulcanization (HTV) and Room Temperature Vulcanization (RTV). HTV silicone is cured at elevated temperatures, offering excellent thermal stability and mechanical properties, making it suitable for demanding applications like automotive and aerospace. Conversely, RTV silicone cures at room temperature, providing ease of use and versatility for general-purpose applications, such as adhesives and sealants. Both types are essential across various industries, catering to specific performance requirements and processing conditions.</p></p>
