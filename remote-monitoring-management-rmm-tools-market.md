<p><p>Remote Monitoring & Management (RMM) tools can be categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based RMM solutions are hosted on the provider's servers, allowing users to access them via the internet, ensuring scalability, automatic updates, and reduced maintenance costs. In contrast, on-premises RMM tools are installed locally on a company's infrastructure, offering greater control over data security and compliance but requiring more resources for installation and management. Each type caters to different organizational needs and preferences.</p></p>
