<p><p>The specialty valves market includes various types that cater to specific applications. Ball valves provide quick on/off control with low pressure drop, making them ideal for fluid handling. Butterfly valves are efficient for regulating flow in large pipes, utilizing a disk that rotates to open or close. Diaphragm valves feature a flexible membrane that seals against a seat, perfect for handling corrosive fluids. Other specialty valves encompass specialized designs, including check valves and pressure relief valves, tailored for unique operational needs.</p></p>
