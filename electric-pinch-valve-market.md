<p><p>Electric pinch valves are devices used to control the flow of liquids and slurries in industrial applications. They utilize an electric actuator to compress a flexible tube, effectively pinching it to open or close the valve, allowing for precise flow regulation. The advantage of electric pinch valves includes minimal leakage, reduced maintenance, and compatibility with a variety of fluids, making them ideal for sectors such as wastewater management, pharmaceuticals, and food processing.</p><p>The Electric Pinch Valve Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.7% during the forecast period.
