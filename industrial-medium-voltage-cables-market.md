<p><p>The industrial medium-voltage cables market comprises three main types: XLPE cables, MI cables, and EPR cables. XLPE (cross-linked polyethylene) cables offer high thermal resistance and electrical insulation, making them suitable for various industrial applications. MI (mineral insulated) cables provide exceptional robustness and fire resistance, ideal for hazardous environments. EPR (ethylene propylene rubber) cables feature excellent flexibility and resistance to heat and moisture, making them versatile for industrial installations. Each type addresses specific operational needs across various sectors.</p></p>
