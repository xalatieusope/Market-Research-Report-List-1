<p><p>The digital cordless phones market comprises three main types based on frequency: 2.4 GHz, 5.8 GHz, and DECT 6.0. The 2.4 GHz phones are widely used but can face interference from other devices like Wi-Fi. The 5.8 GHz phones offer better range and reduced interference but are less common. DECT 6.0 phones operate in a dedicated frequency band, providing superior range, enhanced call quality, and security. Each type caters to different user needs and preferences in cordless communication.</p></p>
