<p><p>Application Lifecycle Management (ALM) encompasses various market types, mainly On-Premise and Hosted solutions. On-Premise ALM involves deploying software directly on the organization's infrastructure, offering enhanced control, data security, and customization. In contrast, Hosted ALM solutions are cloud-based, enabling access from anywhere, reducing maintenance overhead, and often providing scalability and cost-effectiveness. Organizations choose between these types based on their unique needs, budget constraints, and preferences for security and management control. Each type plays a vital role in optimizing the application development process.</p></p>
