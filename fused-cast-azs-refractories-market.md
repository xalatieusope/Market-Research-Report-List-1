AZS33 contains approximately 33% Al₂O₃, providing a balance of thermal stability and mechanical strength. AZS36 features 36% Al₂O₃ for enhanced durability, while AZS41, with 41% Al₂O₃, offers superior resistance to corrosion and thermal shock, making it ideal for demanding applications.</p></p>
