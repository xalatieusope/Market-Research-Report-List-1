<p><p>The Calcium Fluoride Lens market encompasses two primary types: spherical lenses and aspheric lenses. Spherical lenses feature a simple curved surface, providing uniform focus but may introduce optical distortions. In contrast, aspheric lenses have a complex curvature, enabling improved light transmission and reduced aberrations, resulting in superior image quality. Both types are widely used in various applications, including optics, laser technology, and scientific instruments, catering to industries that demand precise optical performance and enhanced clarity.</p></p>
