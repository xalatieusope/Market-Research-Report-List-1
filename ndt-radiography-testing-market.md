<p><strong>The NDT-Radiography Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Film Radiography</li><li>Real Time Radiography (RTR)</li><li>Computed Tomography (CT)</li><li>Digital Radiography (DR)</li><li>Computed Radiography (CR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The NDT-Radiography Testing market comprises several key types: </p><p>1. **Film Radiography** uses traditional film to capture images for evaluating material integrity.</p><p>2. **Real-Time Radiography (RTR)** provides instant imaging, enabling immediate assessment.</p><p>3. **Computed Tomography (CT)** offers cross-sectional images for detailed analysis.</p><p>4. **Digital Radiography (DR)** enables direct digital capture of images, enhancing efficiency and image quality.</p><p>5. **Computed Radiography (CR)** utilizes imaging plates for digital conversion, balancing traditional and modern techniques. Each type caters to diverse industrial inspection needs.</p></p>
