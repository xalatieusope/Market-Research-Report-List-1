<p><p>The Blue Light-emitting Diodes (LEDs) market consists of two primary types: Passive Matrix Organic Light Emitting Diodes (PMOLED) and Active Matrix Organic Light Emitting Diodes (AMOLED). PMOLEDs are simpler and more cost-effective, suitable for smaller displays, but have limitations in resolution and screen size. In contrast, AMOLEDs offer higher resolution, faster response times, and better power efficiency, making them ideal for larger screens like smartphones and TVs. Both technologies leverage blue LEDs for vibrant displays and energy efficiency.</p></p>
