<p><ul><li>Dry Cell</li><li>Alternating Current</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pulse igniter market comprises two main types: dry cell and alternating current (AC) igniters. Dry cell igniters utilize chemical reactions within a battery-like structure to produce electrical pulses, ideal for portable applications and outdoor equipment. In contrast, alternating current igniters rely on power from an AC source, generating sparks through oscillating current, making them suitable for industrial and fixed installations. Both types serve various applications, from automotive to heating systems, highlighting their importance in ignition technology.</p></p>
