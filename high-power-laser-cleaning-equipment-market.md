<p><ul><li>Continuous Laser Cleaner</li><li>Pulsed Laser Cleaner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High Power Laser Cleaning Equipment comprises two main types: Continuous Laser Cleaners and Pulsed Laser Cleaners. Continuous Laser Cleaners emit a steady beam, ideal for surface cleaning over large areas, ensuring consistent removal of contaminants like rust and paint. In contrast, Pulsed Laser Cleaners release short bursts of high-intensity light, effective for precision cleaning and delicate surfaces, minimizing heat transfer. Both types offer eco-friendly alternatives to traditional methods, enhancing efficiency and reducing the environmental impact of cleaning processes.</p></p>
