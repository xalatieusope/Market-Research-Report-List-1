<p><ul><li>AC Film Capacitors</li><li>DC Film Capacitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The EV film capacitors market is divided into two primary types: AC film capacitors and DC film capacitors. AC film capacitors are designed for applications in alternating current systems, providing energy storage, filtering, and power factor correction for electric vehicle (EV) charging systems. Conversely, DC film capacitors are tailored for direct current applications, commonly used in electric drive systems and energy storage. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing the performance, efficiency, and reliability of electric vehicles.</p></p>
