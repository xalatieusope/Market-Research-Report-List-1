<p><p>Cosmetic grade kaolin powder is categorized into three types: hard kaolin, soft kaolin, and sandy kaolin. Hard kaolin, known for its fine particles and high purity, is ideal for applications requiring a cleaner finish, such as face masks and powders. Soft kaolin has a more absorbent nature, making it suitable for sensitive skin formulations. Sandy kaolin contains coarser particles, often used for exfoliating products. Each type plays a crucial role in formulating cosmetic products based on texture and skin compatibility.</p></p>
