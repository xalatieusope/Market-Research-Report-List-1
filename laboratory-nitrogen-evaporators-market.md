<p><strong>The Laboratory Nitrogen Evaporators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Metal Dry Bath</li><li>Water Bath</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Laboratory nitrogen evaporators facilitate the rapid and efficient removal of solvents from samples, primarily using nitrogen gas. The market is segmented into two main types: metal dry baths and water baths. Metal dry baths offer uniform heating through metal blocks, providing precise temperature control, while water baths utilize heated water for gentle and consistent heating. Each type is suited for different applications, with metal dry baths often preferred for temperature-sensitive samples and water baths for larger volumes requiring gradual evaporation.</p></p>
