<p><p>Non-thermal processing technologies enhance food safety and preservation without significant heat application. High Pressure Processing (HPP) utilizes intense pressure to destroy pathogens and extend shelf life. Pulsed Electric Field (PEF) employs short bursts of electric fields to theoretically disrupt microbial cell membranes. Irradiation exposes food to ionizing radiation, minimizing spoilage and microbial growth. Ultrasound employs high-frequency sound waves to enhance extraction and preservation. Cold Plasma uses ionized gas to eliminate microbes and extend shelf life without heat alteration.</p></p>
