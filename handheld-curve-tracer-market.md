<p><p>The handheld curve tracer market comprises two main types: single terminal devices and three terminal devices. Single terminal devices measure the characteristics of electronic components, such as diodes and resistors, by connecting one terminal and observing the output on a display. In contrast, three terminal devices, commonly used for transistors, measure more complex characteristics by connecting three terminals, allowing for comprehensive analysis of current-voltage relationships. Both types serve as essential tools for electronics testing and design in compact formats, promoting portability and convenience.</p></p>
