<p><p>The explosives detection scanner market encompasses various technologies used for identifying explosive materials. Gamma-ray radiography technology utilizes high-energy gamma rays to analyze the composition of suspicious objects. X-ray radiography technology employs X-rays to penetrate items and create images for detection purposes. Muon tomography technology leverages cosmic muons to visualize and determine the density of concealed materials. Each technology offers distinct advantages in terms of sensitivity, depth of penetration, and application environments, catering to security needs across various sectors.</p></p>
