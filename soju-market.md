<p><p>The Soju market can be categorized into two main types: distilled soju and diluted soju. Distilled soju is traditionally produced through fermentation and distillation, resulting in a higher alcohol content and a more refined flavor profile. In contrast, diluted soju is made by mixing distilled alcohol with water, flavorings, or other ingredients, leading to a smoother taste and lower alcohol by volume. Both types cater to varied consumer preferences, influencing their popularity in domestic and international markets.</p></p>
