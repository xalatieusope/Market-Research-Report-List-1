<p><p>Virtual Desktop Infrastructure (VDI) enables users to access desktop environments hosted on servers, allowing centralized management and security. The market comprises two main types: On-Premise VDI, where organizations host and manage their own virtual desktops internally, offering greater control but requiring significant resources and maintenance; and Cloud-Based VDI, where the infrastructure is provided by third-party cloud providers, offering scalability and reduced maintenance for organizations while enabling remote access from various devices. Both cater to different business needs and IT strategies.</p></p>
