<p><p>The Aluminium Brazing Flux market offers two primary types: Powder and Paste. Powder flux is a dry, fine-grained form, ideal for applications requiring precise control and uniform distribution, often used in automated brazing processes. Paste flux, a mixture of powder and a binder, provides better adhesion and is easier to apply manually or in complex joints. Both types enhance wetting, remove oxides, and ensure strong, clean brazed joints, catering to diverse industrial needs based on application requirements and process preferences.</p></p>
