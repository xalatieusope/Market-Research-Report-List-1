<p><p>Thermosetting composites are categorized into three main types based on their reinforcement materials. Short Fiber Reinforced Thermosets (SFRT) utilize short fibers for enhanced mechanical properties, offering good strength and weight efficiency. Long Fiber Reinforced Thermosets (LFRT) incorporate longer fibers, providing superior strength and impact resistance. Glass Fiber Reinforced Thermosets (GFRT) specifically use glass fibers to enhance durability and thermal stability, making them ideal for applications in automotive, aerospace, and construction, where performance and reliability are essential.</p></p>
