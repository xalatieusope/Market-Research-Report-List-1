<p><p>The Micro Electrical Mechanical Systems (MEMS) market encompasses a variety of devices, each serving unique functions. MEMS microphones convert sound into electrical signals, and optical MEMS manipulate light for imaging applications. Pressure sensors gauge environmental pressure, critical for various industries. RF MEMS enable compact radio frequency components, enhancing communication. Other MEMS devices include accelerometers and sensors catering to emerging technologies and specialized applications.</p></p>
