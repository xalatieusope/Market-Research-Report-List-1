<p><p>The Silicon Controlled Rectifiers (SCR) market encompasses two primary types: unidirectional and bidirectional SCRs. Unidirectional SCRs allow current flow in one direction and are commonly used in applications like power control and rectification. In contrast, bidirectional SCRs can conduct current in both directions, making them suitable for alternating current (AC) applications, such as in light dimmers and motor speed controls. The choice between these types depends on the specific application requirements and electrical characteristics.</p></p>
