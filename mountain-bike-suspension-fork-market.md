<p><strong>The Mountain Bike Suspension Fork Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air Spring Suspension Fork</li><li>Coil Spring Suspension Fork</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mountain bike suspension forks are categorized mainly into air spring and coil spring types. Air spring suspension forks utilize compressed air to adjust firmness and responsiveness, offering lightweight performance and easy tuneability for varying terrains. In contrast, coil spring suspension forks rely on steel coils for shock absorption, providing consistent performance and reliability under heavy loads. Each type caters to different riding styles, with air springs favored for versatility and coil springs preferred for their durability and simplicity in maintenance.</p></p>
