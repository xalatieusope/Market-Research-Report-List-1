<p><p>The Medium and High Voltage Testing Market encompasses several key testing types. Sustained Low Frequency Tests evaluate insulation integrity over extended periods under lower frequency conditions. Constant DC Tests assess dielectric strength and insulation performance through continuous direct current. High Frequency Tests focus on materials' behavior under rapid electrical changes. Surge or Impulse Tests simulate electrical surges to analyze equipment behavior during transient events. Each type plays a vital role in ensuring safety and reliability in high voltage electrical systems.</p></p>
