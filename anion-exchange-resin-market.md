<p><p>The anion exchange resin market is primarily divided into Strong Base Anion Resins and Weak Base Anion Resins. Strong Base Anion Resins efficiently remove negatively charged ions, such as sulfate and nitrate, from solutions under a wide pH range, making them suitable for water purification and industrial applications. Weak Base Anion Resins, on the other hand, are effective in removing weakly acidic ions, functioning best in specific pH conditions, typically utilized in specific water treatment processes and food applications.</p></p>
