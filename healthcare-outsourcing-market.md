<p><p>Healthcare outsourcing encompasses two primary market types: product outsourcing and services outsourcing. Product outsourcing involves the external procurement of medical devices, pharmaceuticals, and equipment from specialized manufacturers, ensuring cost efficiency and access to advanced technologies. In contrast, services outsourcing pertains to third-party management of non-core functions like billing, IT support, and medical transcription, allowing healthcare providers to focus on patient care while enhancing operational efficiency. Both strategies aim to optimize resources and improve service delivery in the healthcare sector.</p></p>
