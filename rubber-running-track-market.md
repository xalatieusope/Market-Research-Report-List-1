<p><p>The rubber running track market is categorized into two primary types: natural rubber and synthetic rubber. Natural rubber, derived from rubber trees, offers excellent resilience and cushioning, making it appealing for outdoor tracks. However, it may be less durable under intense weather conditions. Conversely, synthetic rubber, made from petroleum-based materials, is highly durable, weather-resistant, and requires minimal maintenance, making it ideal for both indoor and outdoor facilities. The choice between these types often depends on specific performance and environmental requirements.</p></p>
